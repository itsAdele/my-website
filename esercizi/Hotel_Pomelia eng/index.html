About us!

Tradition and sustainability at the heart of what we do.

Hotel Pomelia is a family-run business that has welcomed travelers to the heart of Ragusa for three generations. Today, the new generation has completely renovated the property, transforming it into an eco-friendly structure that honors both nature and local traditions.

Whether you're planning a relaxing family getaway or a romantic escape, you'll find cozy spaces, authentic experiences, and a true commitment to the land and its people.

Furthermore we are proud to be the first hotel in Sicily to earn Benefit Corporation certification, a testament to our strong commitment to sustainability and the well-being of our community.

How we did it

  • Clean energy

    The entire property is powered by renewable energy, with part of it generated on-site through our solar panels.


  • Unique and local furnishing

    Each room is decorated with furniture made from reclaimed materials, transformed into unique pieces by local artisans and artists.


  • Eco-friendly textiles

    All linens are made from GOTS-certified organic cotton and hemp, a natural fibers that contain no microplastics.


  • Meaningful giving

    All replaced linens have been donated to a local non-profit organization, which distributes them to families in need.

Our services

  • Kitchen with zero-km ingredients from our garden and local producers.

  • Equipped, accessible beach.

  • Guided tours and trekking on request.

  • Cooking workshops and local labs by reservation.
